1. Maximum Likelihood Estimation

1.1 Point Estimation

我们从盒子中等概率i.i.d.的取一个球并放回，借此想要得知取到红球的概率。可知事件的模型为\(f(x;p)=p^x(1-p)^{1-x}\)；我们把取到红球记为1，否则为0；我们一共取了\(n\)球，得到了一串数据\(T=\{X_1=x_1,...,X_n=x_n\}, x_i\in \{0,1\}\)。利用以上信息我们去估计\(p=u(x_1,...,x_n)\)的值，又因为\(p\)是一个点（值），所以称之为点估计

1.2 MLE Method

我们可以先取一个值\(p=p_0\)，此时模型就已知为\(f(x;p_0)\)，我们可以算出\(P(X_1=x_1,...,X_n=x_n;p=p_0)=\prod_{i=1}^{n}p^{x_{i}}(1-p)^{1-x_{i}}=p^{\Sigma x_{i}}(1-p)^{n-\Sigma x_{i}}\)。对\(p\)每一个可能的取值，我们都可以得出一个概率。MLE的想法是，在这些取值中，找使得概率\(P\)最大的\(\hat p\)，作为我们的预测值。

数学上，\(P(X_1=x_1,...,X_n=x_n;p)=\prod_{i=1}^{n}f(x_i;p)=L(p),\;p \in[0,1]\)，\(L(p)\)叫做似然函数 Likelihood function。我们要求函数\(L(p)\)的极大值，记为\(\hat p\)，我们有\(L'(\hat p)=0,\;\;L''(\hat p)<0\)。

由于有累积的存在，直接求一阶导\(L'(p)\)往往比较复杂，i.e \(L(p)=p^{\Sigma x_{i}}(1-p)^{n-\Sigma x_{i}}, \quad 0 \leq p \leq 1\)，所以我们引入log函数，把累积转换成累加，方便导数的计算，i.e. \(\ln L(p)=\left(\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i}\right) \ln p+\left(n-\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i}\right) \ln (1-p)\)。

定义01（MLE）

Let \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\) be a random sample from a distribution that depends on one or more unknown parameters \(\theta_{1}, \theta_{2}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\) with pmf or pdf that is denoted by \(f\left(x ; \theta_{1}, \theta_{2}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\right) .\) Suppose that \(\left(\theta_{1}, \theta_{2}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\right)\) is restricted to a given parameter space \(\Omega .\) Then the joint pmf or pdf of \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n},\) namely, \[ \begin{aligned} L\left(\theta_{1}, \theta_{2}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\right)=f(&\left.x_{1} ; \theta_{1}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\right) f\left(x_{2} ; \theta_{1}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\right) \cdots f\left(x_{n} ; \theta_{1}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\right), \quad\left(\theta_{1}, \theta_{2}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\right) \in \Omega \end{aligned} \] is called the Likelihood function, which is regarded as a function of \(\theta_{1}, \theta_{2}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\)。

Let \(\left[u_{1}\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right), u_{2}\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right), \ldots, u_{m}\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)\right]\) be the \(m\)-tuple in \(\Omega\) that maximizes \(L\left(\theta_{1}, \theta_{2}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\right)\), then ML estimators of \(\theta_{1}, \theta_{2}, \ldots, \theta_{m}\) are \[ \begin{aligned} \widehat{\theta}_{1} &=u_{1}\left(X_{1}, \ldots, X_{n}\right) \\ \widehat{\theta}_{2} &=u_{2}\left(X_{1}, \ldots, X_{n}\right) \\ & \vdots \\ \widehat{\theta}_{m} &=u_{m}\left(X_{1}, \ldots, X_{n}\right) \end{aligned} \] ML estimates are the corresponding observed values of these statistics, namely \[ \begin{aligned} u_{1}\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right), u_{2}\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right), \ldots, u_{m}\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) \end{aligned} \]

  • 很多情况下，estimators (also estimates)是唯一的。
  • \(X\)是未知变量，\(x_i\)是具体的数。
  • 有多个\(\theta\)时，求\(L\)对每一个\(\theta\)的偏导。

2. Method of Moments

我们用模型的矩等于样本的矩，即用经验分布代替总体分布函数。

  • \(k\)th moment of the sample: \(M_{k}=\sum_{i=1}^{n} X_{i}^{k} / n\)
  • \(k\)th moment of the distribution: \(E(X^k)\)
  • 用\(k\)th moment 去构造\(k\)个等式，求解\(k\)个estimates。

Example 1

Suppose that \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\) is a random sample from a gamma distribution with parameters \(\alpha=\theta_{1}\) and \(\beta=\theta_{2},\) where \(\theta_{1}>0, \theta_{2}>0 .\) It is difficult to maximize
\[ \begin{aligned} L\left(\theta_{1}, \theta_{2} ; x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)=\left[\frac{1}{\Gamma\left(\theta_{1}\right) \theta_{2}^{\theta_{1}}}\right]^{n}\left(x_{1} x_{2} \cdots x_{n}\right)^{\theta_{1}-1} \exp \left(-\sum_{i=1}^{n} x_{i} / \theta_{2}\right) \end{aligned} \] As we known, \[ \begin{align} E(X)=\theta_{1} \theta_{2}&=\bar{X}=M_1 \nonumber\\ E[(X-E(X))^2]=\theta_{1} \theta_{2}^{2}&=V=\sum_{i=1}^{n}\left(X_{i}-\bar{X}\right)^{k} / n \tag{1} \end{align} \] The solution of which are \[ \begin{aligned} \widetilde{\theta}_{1}=\frac{\bar{X}^{2}}{V} \quad \text { and } \quad \tilde{\theta}_{2}=\frac{V}{\bar{X}} \end{aligned} \]

  • 等式（1）可以由\(M_2=E(X^2)\)，IID条件以及\(\bar{X}\)的定义推导。

3. Bias

Definition 1 （Bias）

If \(E\left[u\left(X_{1}, X_{2}, X_{n}\right)\right]=\theta,\) then the statistic \(u\left(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\right)\) is called an unbiased estimator of \(\theta .\) Otherwise, it is said to be biased.

回到例子上，我们用取的\(n\)个球来估计概率\(\theta\)，得到对其的估计值\(\hat \theta=u(X_1,...,X_n)\)，一个"好的"（unbiased）的估计是指估计值期望与真实参数期望相等，即\(E(\hat \theta)=\theta\)。

  • 对随机变量\(X\)来说，\(E(X)\)~\(\theta\)
  • 对任意分布，样本方差\(S^{2}=\frac{1}{n-1} \sum_{i=1}^{n}\left(X_{i}-\bar{X}\right)^{2}\)是方差的unbiased estimator。

4. Sufficient Statistics

充分统计量包含了样本的所有信息。就如我们知道期望和方差，就能得出正态分布\(N(u,\sigma^2)\)，而不需要知道采样去得到每一个点的概率。

Definition 2 （sufficient statistics）

A statistic \(t=T(X)\) is sufficient for underlying parameter \(\theta\) precisely if the conditional probability distribution of the data \(X\), given the statistic \(t=T(x)\), does not depend on the parameter \(\theta\)

  • 知道了充分统计量\(t\)，那么样本\(X\)的条件概率分布和参数\(\theta\)无关。

Theorem 1 因子分解定理

Let \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\) denote random variables with joint pdf or \(\operatorname{pmf} f\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n} ; \theta\right),\) which depends on the parameter \(\theta .\) The statistic \(Y=u\left(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\right)\) is sufficient for \(\theta\) if and only if \[ f\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n} ; \theta\right)=\phi\left[u\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) ; \theta\right] h\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) \tag{2} \] where \(\phi\) depends on \(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\) only through \(u\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)\) and \(h\left(x_{1}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)\) does not depend on \(\theta\)

  • 对充分统计量做one-to-one变换后，依然是充分统计量。

    • 设\(W=v(Y)=v\left[u\left(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\right)\right]\)为一对一函数，\(Y=v^{-1}(W)\)为逆函数，（2）变成 \[ f\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n} ; \theta\right)=\phi\left[v^{-1}\left\{v\left[u\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)\right]\right\} ; \theta\right] h\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) \nonumber \]

  • 当参数的MLE唯一时（指参数\(\theta_i,\,i=1\)），MLE是充分统计量的函数。

    • 求MLE，即对（1）求对参数\(\theta\)极大值，即对函数\(\phi\)求极值，又\(\phi\sim u\), 所以结果是充分统计量\(u\)的函数。

Example 2

\(X_1,...,X_n\) are random sample from \(b(1,p),p\in[0,1]\), let \(Y=X_1+...+X_n\), \(Y \sim b(n,p)\). By theorem 1, we get \(Y\) is sufficient for \(p\). Only consider when \(y=\sum_{i=1}^nx_i\), the conditional probability is \[ \begin{aligned} P\left(X_{1}=x_{1}, \ldots, X_{n}=x_{n} \mid Y=y\right)&=\frac{P\left(X_{1}=x_{1}, \ldots, X_{n}=x_{n}\right)}{P(Y=y)}\\ &=\frac{p^{x_{1}}(1-p)^{1-x_{1}} \cdots p^{x_{n}}(1-p)^{1-x_{n}}}{\left(\begin{array}{l} n \\ y \end{array}\right) p^{y}(1-p)^{n-y}}\\ &=\frac{1}{\left(\begin{array}{l} n \\ y \end{array}\right)} \end{aligned} \] which does not depend on the parameter \(p\).

  • 我们只要知道抓的\(n\)求中有\(y\)个红球，就能知道抓取球的概率分布。

定理一找充分统计量的方法可以写成exponential form，从乘法转换为加法，方便计算。

Theorem 2

Let \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\) be a random sample from a distribution with a pdf or pmf of the exponential form \[ f(x ; \theta)=\exp [K(x) p(\theta)+S(x)+q(\theta)] \nonumber \] on a support free of \(\theta .\) Then the statistic \(\sum_{i=1}^{n} K\left(X_{i}\right)\) is sufficient for \(\theta\).

  • 不需要写成似然函数的形式，但是结果的累加是由似然函数中的累乘得出的。

  • 当有多个参数\(\theta\)时，分别对应。

    • 假设\(X_1,...,X_n\)独立同分布，\(X\sim N(\theta_1,\;\theta_2)\)

    \[ f\left(x ; \theta_{1}, \theta_{2}\right)=\exp \left(\frac{-1}{2 \theta_{2}} x^{2}+\frac{\theta_1}{\theta_2}x -\frac{\theta_{1}^{2}}{2 \theta_{2}}-\ln \sqrt{2 \pi \theta_{2}}\right) \nonumber \]

    ​ 得出充分统计量分别为 \[ Y_{1}=\sum_{i=1}^{n} X_{i}^{2} \quad \text { and } \quad Y_{2}=\sum_{i=1}^{n} X_{i} \nonumber \]

5. Unbiased 与 Sufficient Statistics

Theorem 2 Rao-Blackwell Theorem

Let \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\) be a random sample from a distribution with pdf or pmf \(f(x ; \theta), \theta \in \Omega .\) Let \(Y_{1}=u_{1}\left(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\right)\) be a sufficient statistic for \(\theta,\) and let \(Y_{2}=u_{2}\left(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\right)\) be an unbiased estimator of \(\theta,\) where \(Y_{2}\) is not a function of \(Y_{1}\) alone. Then \(E\left(Y_{2} \mid y_{1}\right)=u\left(y_{1}\right)\) defines a statistic \(u\left(Y_{1}\right),\) a function of the sufficient statistic \(Y_{1}\), which is an unbiased estimator of \(\theta,\) and its variance is less than that of \(Y_{2}\)

  • 根据sufficient statistics去更新unbiased estimator，得到一个方差更小的unbiased estimator。

  • \(Y_2\)不一定要是无偏的。

  • 可以从一个简单的estimator出发，用定理进行迭代，得到一个better的estimator。
