[Statistics] 3. Tests of Statistical Hypotheses

Posted on 2021-01-13 Edited on 2021-01-14 In study , Campus Views: Valine:
Symbols count in article: 6.3k Reading time ≈ 6 mins.

假设检验 hypothesis testing的核心就是，小概率事件不会发生。

1. Tests about One Mean

1.1 Hypothesis Testing

我们通过process I得到结果为\(X\sim N(u=50,\sigma^2)\)，我们想要证明新的process II后，\(X\sim N(u=55,\sigma^2)\)。这种想用新分布去反驳旧分布的技术成为假设检验。

  • 机器的效率一开始时符合\(X\sim N(u=50,\sigma^2)\)，现在引进新技术，想知道新技术有没有提高效率，即有没有变成\(X\sim N(u=55,\sigma^2)\)。

We want to test the simple null hypothesis \(H_{0}: \mu=50\) against the simple alternative hypothesis \(H_{1}: \mu=55\)。为了进行判断，我们想要从样本\(X_1,...,X_n\)中根据小概率事件不会发生的原则，找出一个规则去接受或拒绝\(H_0\)。针对样本空间来讲，即对样本空间进行一个划分，分成拒绝域critical region \(C=\{(x_1,...,x_n):f(x)\;with\;condition\;\}\)以及接受域\(C'\)。对均值的检验，我们可以用样本均值\(\bar{X}\)作为Test statistic去作为样本的函数去划分域。

  1. Type I error \(\alpha\) $=P$： \(\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) \in C\)，样本在拒绝域上但其实\(H_0\)是对的。
    • \(\alpha\)也称之为 significance level of the test，在检验的开始由我们决定。
  2. Type II error \(\beta\) $=P$： \(\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) \in C'\)，样本在接受域上但其实\(H_1\)是对的。
  • \(C'\)是\(C\)的补集。
  • \(\alpha+\beta\neq1\)
  • \(\alpha\)和\(\beta\)是负相关。\(\alpha,\beta\)分别与样本大小\(n\)负相关。

Type I 和 II error是小概率事件发生了的情况。我们一般认为，当\(H_0\)正确的时候，\(\left(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\right) \in C\)属于小概率事件，不会发生（这也是假设检验的核心），所以我们会犯错。

  • \(H_0\)或是\(H_1\)对是客观的事实，所以存在事实是对的但我们判断成负的概率，反之也是。因此，我们不可能同时犯两种错误。

在检验中，我们有2种方法去view假设检验：

  1. \(p-value\) 可以根据样本的统计量（i.e. \(\bar{X}\)）得到样本对应的\(p-value\)，把p值与\(\alpha\)进行比较也可以得出结果。
  2. confidence intervals

Example 1

1.2 单边、多边检验

当方差可知的时候 \[ \begin{array}{ccc} H_{0} & H_{1} & \text { Critical Region } \\ \hline \mu=\mu_{0} & \mu>\mu_{0} & z \geq z_{\alpha} \text { or } \bar{x} \geq \mu_{0}+z_{\alpha} \sigma / \sqrt{n} \\ \mu=\mu_{0} & \mu<\mu_{0} & z \leq-z_{\alpha} \text { or } \bar{x} \leq \mu_{0}-z_{\alpha} \sigma / \sqrt{n} \\ \mu=\mu_{0} & \mu \neq \mu_{0} & |z| \geq z_{\alpha / 2} \text { or }\left|\bar{x}-\mu_{0}\right| \geq z_{\alpha / 2} \sigma / \sqrt{n} \end{array} \] 但方差位置且样本量\(n<30\)时 \[ \begin{array}{ccc} H_{0} & H_{1} & \text { Critical Region } \\ \hline \mu=\mu_{0} & \mu>\mu_{0} & t \geq t_{\alpha}(n-1) \text { or } \bar{x} \geq \mu_{0}+t_{\alpha}(n-1) s / \sqrt{n} \\ \mu=\mu_{0} & \mu<\mu_{0} & t \leq-t_{\alpha}(n-1) \text { or } \bar{x} \leq \mu_{0}-t_{\alpha}(n-1) s / \sqrt{n} \\ \mu=\mu_{0} & \mu \neq \mu_{0} & |t| \geq t_{\alpha / 2}(n-1) \text { or }\left|\bar{x}-\mu_{0}\right| \geq t_{\alpha / 2}(n-1) s / \sqrt{n} \end{array} \]

2. Power of A Statistical Test

对一个test: \(H_0:p=a,\;H_1:p>a\)来讲，模型为\(b(1,p)\)，当我们选定好一个拒绝域\(C\)的时候，我们找到一个函数\(K(p)\)去联系\(\alpha\)与\(\beta\)。

设有20个样本\(X_1,...,X_{20}\)根据定义可知 \[ \begin{aligned} \alpha&=P(X\in C;P=a)\\ \beta&=P(X\in C';P=b>a)\\ &=1-P(X\in C;P=b) \end{aligned} \] 我们记power function of the test为 \[ \begin{align} K(p)=P(X\in C:P=p),\;\;\;\;p\geq a \end{align} \] 因此我们可以根据power function来表示\(\alpha,\beta\) \[ \begin{aligned} \alpha&=K(p=a)\\ \beta&=1-K(p=b) \end{aligned} \]

  • \(K(p)\sim C,\;p\)

当我们更换拒绝域\(C\)的时候，\(K\)以及\(\alpha,\beta\)也随之变化。因为我们要同时减小两个error \(\alpha,\beta\)，所以我们可以根据power function选择更好的拒绝域\(C\)。

3. Best Critical Regions

拒绝域在满足\(\alpha=P\left(C ; \theta_{0}\right)\)的情况下，可以\(u(X_1,...,X_n)<a,\;u\in [a,b],\;u>b\)。我们要找到一个最好的拒绝域（确定方向）。

定义1 the best critical regions

Consider the test of the simple null hypothesis \(H_{0}: \theta=\theta_{0}\) against the simple alternative hypothesis \(H_{1}: \theta=\theta_{1} .\) Let \(C\) be a critical region of size \(\alpha ;\) that is, \(\alpha=P\left(C ; \theta_{0}\right) .\) Then \(C\) is a best critical region of size \(\alpha\) if, for every other critical region D of size $ =P(D ; _{0}),$ we have \[ P\left(C ; \theta_{1}\right) \geq P\left(D ; \theta_{1}\right) \] That is, when \(H_{1}: \theta=\theta_{1}\) is true, the probability of rejecting \(H_{0}: \theta=\theta_{0}\) with the use of the critical region \(C\) is at least as great as the corresponding probability with the use of any other critical region \(D\) of size \(\alpha\).

  • 在\(\alpha\)一定下，最优拒绝域也是那个有最优power function的定义域。

定理1 Neyman-Pearson Lemma

(Neyman-Pearson Lemma) Let \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\) be a random sample of size \(n\) from a distribution with pdf or \(\operatorname{pmf} f(x ; \theta),\) where \(\theta_{0}\) and \(\theta_{1}\) are two possible values of \(\theta\) Denote the joint pdf or pmf of \(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\) by the likelihood function \[ L(\theta)=L\left(\theta ; x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)=f\left(x_{1} ; \theta\right) f\left(x_{2} ; \theta\right) \cdots f\left(x_{n} ; \theta\right) \] If there exist a positive constant \(k\) and a subset \(C\) of the sample space such that （a） \(P\left[\left(X_{1}, X_{2}, \ldots, X_{n}\right) \in C ; \theta_{0}\right]=\alpha\) （b） \(\frac{L\left(\theta_{0}\right)}{L\left(\theta_{1}\right)} \leq k\) for \(\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) \in C,\) and （c） \(\frac{L\left(\theta_{0}\right)}{L\left(\theta_{1}\right)} \geq k\) for \(\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) \in C^{\prime}\) then \(C\) is a best critical region of size \(\alpha\) for testing the simple null hypothesis \(H_{0}\) :\(\theta=\theta_{0}\) against the simple alternative hypothesis \(H_{1}: \theta=\theta_{1}\)

  • \(K\)是常数，根据\(\alpha\)的变化而变化
  • 得出的方向是不变的

定义2 Uniformly most powerful test

\(8.6-2\) A test defined by a critical region \(C\) of size \(\alpha\) is a uniformly most powerful test if it is a most powerful test against each simple alternative in \(H_{1} .\) The critical region \(C\) is called a uniformly most powerful critical region of size \(\alpha\).

  • ？

  • 当充分统计量存在时，最优拒绝域与uniformly power critical region取决于充分统计量。

    • \(\begin{aligned} \frac{L\left(\theta_{0}\right)}{L\left(\theta_{1}\right)} &=\frac{\phi\left[u\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) ; \theta_{0}\right] h\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)}{\phi\left[u\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) ; \theta_{1}\right] h\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right)} \\ &=\frac{\phi\left[u\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) ; \theta_{0}\right)}{\phi\left[u\left(x_{1}, x_{2}, \ldots, x_{n}\right) ; \theta_{1}\right]} \end{aligned}\)

4. Likelihood Ratio Tests 似然比检验

似然比检验：\(H_0\)和\(H_1\)是composite，即\(H_{0}: \theta \in \omega, \quad H_{1}: \theta \in \omega^{\prime}\)，\(\omega\bigcup w'= \Omega\)

N-P lemma：\(H_0,H_1\)是simple的 i.e. \(H_0=a,H_1=b\)。

定义3 似然比 Likelihood

The likelihood ratio is the quotient \[ \lambda=\frac{L(\widehat{\omega})}{L(\widehat{\Omega})} \] where \(L(\widehat{\omega})\) is the maximum of the likelihood function with respect to \(\theta\) when \(\theta \in \omega\) and \(L(\widehat{\Omega})\) is the maximum of the likelihood function with respect to \(\theta\) when \(\theta \in \Omega\)

  • \(\lambda\)是一个关于充分统计量的分布。

定义4 似然比检验

To test \(H_{0}: \theta \in \omega\) against \(H_{1}: \theta \in \omega^{\prime},\) the critical region for the likelihood ratio test is the set of points in the sample space for which \[ \lambda=\frac{L(\widehat{\omega})}{L(\widehat{\Omega})} \leq k \] where \(0<k<1\) and \(k\) is selected so that the test has a desired significance level \(\alpha\)
